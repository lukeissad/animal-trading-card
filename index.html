Red squirrel

In Norse mythology, Ratatoskr is a red squirrel who runs up and down with messages in the world tree, Yggdrasil, and spreads gossip.

  • Scientific name: Sciurus vulgaris
  • Average lenght: 19 to 23 cm
  • Average lifespan: 5 years
  • Habitat: Forests

The red squirrel has a typical head-and-body length of 19 to 23 cm, a tail length of 15 to 20 cm. The long tail helps the squirrel to balance and steer when jumping from tree to tree and running along branches, and may keep the animal warm during sleep.
